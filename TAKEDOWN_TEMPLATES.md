# Takedown Templates

These are practical templates. Replace bracketed fields before sending.

## A) Platform/App Store Notice (DMCA-style)

Subject: Copyright Infringement Notice - [Infringing App Name]

To [Platform/Provider Team],

I am the copyright owner (or authorized agent) for the software known as "ValuePics".

Original work details:
- Title: ValuePics
- Owner: Wally Horsman, Queensland Australia
- Contact: wrhorsman@gmail.com
- Evidence of ownership: [repo/release URLs], NOTICE and LICENSE files, dated release artifacts

Infringing material details:
- Infringing app/listing: [URL]
- Developer/account: [name]
- Package/bundle ID: [id]
- Specific copied elements: [code text/UI assets/strings/screenshots]

Requested action:
Please remove or disable access to the infringing material identified above.

Required statements:
- I have a good-faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
- The information in this notice is accurate.
- Under penalty of perjury (where applicable), I am the owner, or authorized to act on behalf of the owner, of the exclusive rights allegedly infringed.

Signature:
[Full Legal Name]
[Date]
[Contact Email]

## C) Initial Cease-and-Desist Email (non-court)

Subject: Unauthorized Use of Copyrighted Software (ValuePics)

Hello [Name/Team],

I am the copyright owner of ValuePics. It has come to my attention that your app/content at [URL] appears to copy protected elements of my software.

Please do the following within [7] days:
1. Remove the infringing content/app/listing.
2. Stop further distribution and updates of the infringing material.
3. Confirm in writing that removal is complete.

If this is not resolved promptly, I may escalate through the platform takedown process and seek further remedies.

Regards,
Wally Horsman
wrhorsman@gmail.com

## Notes

- Keep language factual and professional.
- Do not exaggerate claims.
- Keep all sent copies and provider ticket IDs.
- If significant commercial harm is involved, consult a lawyer in your jurisdiction.
